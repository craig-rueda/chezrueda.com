What could a Life Coach help you achieve?

Change

Whether it’s exploring your current options, needing to make a decision, preparing for change or transition, support through a big life move or change or challenging times in your relationships. Our Life Coaches will help you clarify exactly what you want and help you make the change.

Relationships

Successful relationships are key to a happy, satisfying and successful life, for all of us. Life Coaching is a great way to develop your interpersonal skills, design how you want to be in your relationships and find new ways to be more successful with everyone you come into contact with.

Balance

Achieving true balance is both an art and a science, feeling backed up and supported to be selective is one of the greatest benefits a Life Coach will give you in achieving your version of a balanced life. Enhance the quality of your time at home, at work, with family and loved ones.

Confidence

Your confidence isn’t static, it’s up and down in different aspects of your life depending on your circumstances at different times, having your own dedicated Life Coach to ensure you’re feeling confident in the right ways and able to live your life to the full is an area we specialise in.

About Me

As a coach, mom, wife, HR professional, survivor of postpartum and anxiety, I understand how it feels when life throws everything at you all at once. After trial and error with various methods to cope with feeling overwhelmed, I embraced habit change that significantly shifted my perspective on life, allowing me to lose unwanted weight, incorporate movement back into my life and release some of the extreme internal demands I was putting on myself to be perfect.

My years spent in industries such as beauty, retail customer service, Executive Support and People Operations have engrained in me this intense desire to be in-service to others. It is this desire that has led me to the coaching profession to help others find their path to wellness and well-being. I hold three certifications from the Health Coach Institute: Health and Wellness Coaching, Life Coaching and Advanced Nutrition as well as a B.S. in Business Administration and Management from Notre Dame de Namur University.

Everyone’s journey is different, unique. I thrive on finding that uniqueness in everyone I meet (including people in the line at the grocery store, which drives my husband nuts!) This passion for discovery allows me to uncover what truly motivates people so that we can bring about lasting change in their lives.
